This meetup is active in the summer when the cruise ships are in Vancouver. We do ship tours between the disembarkation and embarkation process. The cruise lines provide us with these tours to view their ships and sample their fantastic cuisine. They typically provide us special future cruise offers while onboard. Boarding the ship for the tours requires passage through port security and all participants must be cleared in advance with ID information (driver's license or passport). To register, you must follow the instructions closely to be placed on the ship's manifest.

Newest Meetup: Norwegian Sun - Ship Tour & Lunch

Come and see what freestyle cruising onboard Norwegian is all about. This is a perfect ship for first time cruisers and multi-generational families. There are limited spots available on this ship tour and they always fill quickly. A gourmet three course lunch in the main dining room with wine is included.

Register here: Cruise Lover's Meetup

IMPORTANT REGISTRATION INFORMATION:

  • Boarding the ship for this tour requires passage through port security.
  • All participants must be cleared in advance with ID information from your driver's license or passport.
  • You must bring this same ID with you to the port on the date of the tour.
  • An email will follow your RSVP to collect your ID information.
  • You are not confirmed until you are registered on the ship's manifest.
  • The first completed registrations receive the available spots.
  • The ship tour and lunch is in the ship's currency of US dollars.
